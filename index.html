What is a doula?

A doula is a trained companion who accompanies you during pregnancy and throughout the birthing process. Experienced at childbirth, I provide emotional and physical support to you and your birth partner. I help you prepare mentally for your upcoming birth, and support you in creating a birth plan for your care providers that encapsulates your very own vision for this special day. During labor I stay at your side, helping with breathing & relaxation techniques, and when necessary can help you create room in your body to allow baby to occupy an optimal position for birth. After baby's arrival earthside I support you with breastfeeding and bonding.
